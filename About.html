About Us - Speedo EV Bikes

Since 2000, Speedo EV Bikes has been at the forefront of two-wheeler innovation in Pakistan. With over two decades of experience, we’ve built a legacy of trust, performance, and forward-thinking technology. Today, we proudly operate one of the largest electric bike manufacturing facilities in Pakistan, equipped with state-of-the-art technology and a passionate team committed to excellence. Our flagship models — including the iconic Speedo Ninja — represent the perfect fusion of style, durability, and eco-friendly performance. Designed for Pakistani roads and powered by advanced electric systems, Speedo bikes offer riders a cleaner, smarter, and more cost-effective way to move. With a strong dealer network, after-sales service, and continuous innovation, Speedo EV is not just a brand — it’s a movement. We are proud to be recognized as one of the most trusted EV brands in the country, delivering what many call the best electric bikes in Pakistan. Join the thousands who have already made the switch. Ride with Speedo. Ride the future.
