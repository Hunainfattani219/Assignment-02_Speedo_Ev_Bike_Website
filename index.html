The Price of Petrol vs. Electric Bikes

A 70cc bike typically travels 53 km/l, a 125cc bike 45 km/l, and a 150cc bike 35 km/l. Given that petrol cost is Rs. 268 in Pakistan today, the cost of a:


  • Rs. 5.2 is 70cc.

  • 125cc is equivalent to Rs. 6.2.

  • 9.6 is 150cc.



These days, an EV scooter or motorcycle typically has a motor with 800–1600 watts and a battery life of 23–50 Ah, giving it a range of 60–90 km. This indicates that they will need 0.5-2 units of power. Calculating all the commutation costs, an EV costs between Rs. 1.5 and 2.5 per km, which is significantly less than the cost of a two-wheeler fuel vehicle.
